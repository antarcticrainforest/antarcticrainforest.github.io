Photo Uploader Cli (Personal)

This desktop script converts my Canon RAW photos to JPEG and uploads them to my Google Photos account into an app-created album. It is a personal tool; it is not a commercial service and has no server backend.

What the app does

  • Reads local JPEG files on my computer.
  • Uploads them to Google Photos using the Photos Library API.
  • Creates/uses an app-created album.
  • (Optional) Sets a text description on items the app uploaded.

What the app does NOT do

  • No access to user-created or shared albums outside the appâ€™s own album(s).
  • No pixel editing inside Google Photos; edits are done locally before upload.
  • No selling or sharing of data with third parties.
  • No server-side data storage.
